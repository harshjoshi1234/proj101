Built a multi-visual Power BI dashboard on a real-world SaaS sales dataset (9,994 records) to derive actionable business insights.
Key insights surfaced:
	•	Finance and Energy are the highest revenue generating industries
	•	EMEA leads all regions in total sales volume
	•	SMB segment contributes 50%+ of total revenue
	•	ContactMatcher and FinanceHub are top performing products
Tools used: Microsoft Power BI, Microsoft Excel (data cleaning, VLOOKUP, Pivot Tables)
Dataset: B2B SaaS Sales (global transactions 2020–2023)
